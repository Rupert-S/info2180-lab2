September 29, 2021 by Rupert Scott

Prior to highschool i had no interest in the field until i made my first program using pascal. This program was an employee management system which ended up becoming my School Based Assessment (SBA) for my CXCs.

Even after how happy I was working on and completing that program for my SBA I still was yet to be convinced that that was to be my path in life I was stuck on my childhood dream of becoming a Veterinarian and it would take college to for me to make the change to be where I am now. I went on doing all the core courses nneded to pursue a Veterinarian degree and after going into a class was not schedule to have and one of my friends aiming for a computer science degree was stuck on a problem after multiple attempts we both were at a loss but I was still happy and interested to help her. So, after a day of the both of us trying we finally came upon a way to achieve the needed results for the program and I was overjoyed which was a feeling I had not been feeling in the courses I was doing at the time I then said to myself at that moment this is something I would do everyday of my life.
